## What to Expect

We'll be publishing content in a few categories:

- **Engineering** — deep dives into how things work under the hood

## Writing Blog Posts

Blog posts are Markdown files in the `content/blog/` directory. Each file uses the naming pattern `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md` and includes frontmatter at the top:

```yaml
---
title: My Post Title
description: A short summary of the post.
date: 2026-01-15
category: Tutorial
authors:
  - name: Jane Doe
    role: Technical Writer
---
```

Everything above the `{/* truncate */}` marker becomes the post excerpt shown on the blog landing page.
